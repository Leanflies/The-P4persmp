    Pre-Season 1

    When C!Taylor was young they grew up with C!Rachel and their mum for a while however ended up being sent to prison for thieving. C!Ethan had caught them stealing food from his parents' stall and reported it because he thought that was the right thing to do. When C!Taylor was in prison they had a situationship with another inmate whose sentence was increased because they covered for C!Taylor after they started a fight. C!Taylor was also cursed by a witch with lightning and that caused them to grow horns out of their head (no, they aren't papier-mache)

    Season 1

    At the beginning of the season C!Taylor reconnects with C!Rachel after going to prison and meets C!Andy. They later are part of the party that saves C!Ethan from dying by getting him a rotten spellbook from the catacombs. They were tasked to send invitations to everyone for the Halloween party where C!Ember met everyone. They attended the New Year's Eve party under the alias of "Robin" and after the party raided the palace with C!Ethan and C!Desca. Once they had robbed the palace, C!Ethan and them got drunk and C!Ethan admitted his feelings for C!Andy. C!Taylor goes on an adventure with C!Rachel and C!Andy and afterwards C!Andy explains what happened at the tavern so C!Taylor becomes aware of both sides of the situationship. C!Taylor loses their backpack in a river and goes to C!Ethan for fishing stuff. The two end up hiding the loot from the palace underneath Grimwyck as the Giredale guards are going knocking on doors.

    They run the bookshop in Giredale which they took after finding it abandoned. When adventuring they collect books (both normal and enchanted) in order to sell them, as well as scrolls and ink. They learn that they haven't been paying the rent on her bookshop but rather C!Fire has been paying it for them. They are suspicious of C!Ashlyn after they discover her secret room where she studies faires and asks C!Ethan about what she gets up to.
