    Pre-Season 1

    When C!Andy was a child they weren't able to do magic unlike their mum and sister (C!Rachel) which led to them trying to prove to both themself and their mother that they were worthy of love. C!Andy went to a haunted house but ended up not being able to fight the witch and a black cat (Doris) stepped in and used her magic to save C!Andy. However C!Andy got caught in her magic and to this day has golden scars up their arms which they keep hidden under bandages. Their dad found them on the edge of the forest and told them it was all just a nightmare. Not long after their mother ran off and took C!Rachel with her. The twins spent the rest of their childhood seperated, writing letters to one another however at some point the letters stopped reaching the other.

    Season 1

    At the beginning of season 1 we see C!Andy's dad and them of studying trees. C!Andy's dad sends the home and tells them to continue his work and take over the business. C!Andy's hatred for C!Ethan is shown early when they are reconnecting with their sister. They are part of the group that go to the catacombs to help C!Ethan get his spellbook back but argue it is for the protection of C!AShlyn and C!Taylor. Eventually they befriend C!Ethan and become very close with him. After a christmas party, C!Ethan confesses to them and they say that things are "moving too fast". At the New Year's Eve party, they attend under the alias of "Angel" accompanied by Doris who gives them magical wings that allow them to fly and spend the night with "Lonan". The two almost kiss however Lonan stops it for his feelings for another person and C!Andy leaves before the spell that hides their identities is lifted. At a Valentine's party, Angel was meant to meet Lonan again however he didn't show up. Instead they end up spending the evening with C!Ethan. The two end up very drunk and C!Ethan kisses them. However they end up fighting and C!Andy basically slut shames C!Ethan. C!Andy ends up swapping bodies with C!Ethan in a dream and goes on an adventure with him.

    C!Andy has a complicated relationship with their sister. When they reconnect they are very protective over C!Rachel and refuses to let them do certain things. After C!Andy returns from the catacombs they get into a fight with C!Rachel because C!Andy refused to let them go. C!Andy worries when C!Rachel starts learning magic because they know what damage it can cause. C!Andy suffers badly from nightmares due to what they have experienced with magic and also gets migraines when travelling by waystone or C!Ethan's teleportation magic. They have a strict no hunting rule in their forest however find a cub beside its dead mother. They take the cub in and raise it. They name him Koda however he doesn't grow until after he has hibernated.
