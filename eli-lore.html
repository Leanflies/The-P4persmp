    C!Eli grew up in the north of Aldwynn however ran away due to a disaster. He made it to Coalhaven at age 12 and had to fend for himself.

    C!Eli very quickly became friends with C!Taylor after venturing into Giredale. The two became "pilot pals" as C!Taylor had a plane which C!Eli then stole. He is often out adventuring doing quests to try get by or is off at pilot school. He attended the New Year's Eve party under the alias of "Voyager"
