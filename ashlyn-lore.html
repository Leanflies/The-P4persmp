    Pre-Season 1

    C!Ashlyn grew up at the Snoring Dragon's Tavern, her mother being more active than her father. She was never taught how to run the tavern as her mother married into the business and didn't know how to run it. She spent a lot of time out on dragon studies and received many letters from her parents. On one of her adventures, she met Sheldon who let her shelter under his wing whilst a thunderstorm passed. This was how she bonded with fire dragons and befriended Sheldon. However one day the letters stopped and when C!Ashlyn returned to Grymwyck she found her parents dead.

    Season 1

    C!Ashlyn builds the first known nether portal, originally inside her tavern then moves it to a tower just outside of Grimwyck. She leads a group of people to help save C!Ethan from dying by building a wayward compass to locate the catacombs. She runs the Snoring Dragon's Tavern and C!Ethan has been trying to teach her how to cook. She hosted the Valentine's party however had to leave due to a dragon sighting nearby as she opened the dragon museum in Giredale. She attended the New Year's Eve party under the alias of "Rose". She has a secret room in her tavern where she studies fairies.
