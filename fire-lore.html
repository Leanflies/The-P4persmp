    Pre-Season 1

    C!Fire used to be good friends with C!Andy when they were kids. C!Andy sponsored her to start selling her artworks. She took these artworks outside of Aldwynn and ended up becoming super rich because of it.

    Season 1

    C!Fire returned at the Christmas episode as she was selling her paintings at the market. She has a connection with C!Taylor and has been living in her bookshop as well as paying C!Taylor's rent. She's also a fox.
