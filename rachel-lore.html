    Pre-Season 1

    When C!Rachel was young her father was often out working so she was at home with her mum and brother (C!Andy) a lot. Her mother had the ability to do magic and it soon became known that C!Rachel could also do magic. This led to her mother spending a lot of time teaching her how to do magic. At some point her mother left her father and ran away with C!Rachel. At one point C!Taylor began to live with them and C!Rachel's mum treated C!Taylor like her own. C!Rachel often wrote letters to C!Andy however at some point the responses from C!Andy stopped so they stopped writing to them. After their mother died, C!Rachel moved back to Giredale and took over her aunt's flower shop.

    Season 1

    At the beginning of the season C!Rachel reconnects with C!Andy after not seeing or hearing from them in multiple years. When C!Ashlyn tries to recruit people to go help C!Ethan, they agreed to go but C!Andy refused to let them. They received a letter from C!Andy before they went to the catacombs and when C!Andy returned and stopped by their flower shop, they ended up arguing in the street about how C!Andy controls C!Rachel's life. After speaking with C!Ethan they started relearning magic and with C!Ethan's help became able to use evoker magic. She attended the New Year's Eve party under the alias of "Ren".
